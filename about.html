I want to save the planet from climate change.

I plan to use my programming, website design, graphic design and advocacy skills to urge people to work to end climate change.

Throughout my entire life I've always wanted to preserve the environment. When I reached high school, I joined the school robotics team, science team, and the da Vinci STEAM program. Through these experiences I have developed a love for science, art and research. In the future I want to continue learning about environmental science and climate change to be able to make a difference in the community. Now I hope to use my marketing skills to convince people to reduce their carbon footpints and help to save the planet.
